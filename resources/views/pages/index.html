About Us

Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize funeral services in Zambia, Matswabi Funeral App combines traditional values with modern technology to provide comprehensive support during difficult times.

Our Mission

To provide dignified, stress-free funeral arrangements while creating sustainable employment opportunities for young Zambians. We believe in empowering our community while delivering exceptional service.

Our Impact

Through our innovative approach, we've helped hundreds of families navigate the funeral process while creating jobs and building stronger communities across Lusaka.
